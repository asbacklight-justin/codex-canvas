# Built-in background artwork

[简体中文](README.zh-CN.md) | **English**

These five 1920×1080 JPEG backgrounds were created with Codex's built-in image generation mode for Codex Canvas 1.0.0. They are original project assets rather than third-party downloads.

Prompt set:

- `technology.jpg` — cinematic futuristic technology landscape, neon blue and violet horizon, elegant circuitry and distant server-city forms, dark calm center for readable UI, no text, logo, or watermark, wide 16:9 wallpaper.
- `anime.jpg` — original anime-inspired blue-hour railway station scene with one original young traveler, dreamy city lights and restrained colors, calm open center for UI, no franchise characters, text, logo, or watermark, wide 16:9 wallpaper.
- `city.jpg` — sophisticated midnight city skyline after rain, blue and magenta reflections, cinematic depth, uncluttered dark center for UI, no text, logo, or watermark, wide 16:9 wallpaper.
- `nature.jpg` — tranquil alpine valley at dawn with mist, lake, pine forest, distant snow mountains and subtle warm light, calm center for UI, no text, logo, or watermark, wide 16:9 wallpaper.
- `animal.jpg` — photorealistic red fox resting in a mossy forest at dawn, gentle light rays and shallow depth of field, open dark center for UI, no text, logo, or watermark, wide 16:9 wallpaper.
